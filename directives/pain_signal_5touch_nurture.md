## 5-Touch Sequence (12-14 Days)

### Touch 0 (Day 0): Prospect Fills Onboarding Form
**Trigger:** Cold email prospect responded â†’ Agreed to fill form â†’ Form submitted

**What happens:**
- Prospect shows interest (replied to your cold email)
- Sends them onboarding form link
- They fill it out completely
- Form data captured

**No action needed** - This is the trigger event

---

### Touch 1 (Day 1): FREE Intro Offer - SEND ACTUAL INTROS

**Objective:** "No value, no talk" - DO the work first, send 5-8 actual warm intros

**What to do:**

1. Research 10-15 companies with active pain signals
   - Match client's ICP (titles, industries, company size, location)
   - Find PAIN SIGNALS (job postings, funding, expansions)
   - Deep research each prospect (decision maker name, email, company context)

2. Create 10-15 personalized intro emails
   - Write from client's perspective
   - Reference specific pain signal
   - Include client's value props from form

3. SEND 5-8 intros immediately
   - Email prospects on behalf of client
   - Save 5-7 for Touch 3 (if client engages)

4. Notify client via email
   - Subject: "{FirstName} - just sent 5 warm intros to {ICP titles}"
   - List all prospects contacted
   - Explain pain-signal targeting approach

**Deliverables (.md files):**
- `touch_1_intro_emails.md` (5-8 intro emails sent)
- `touch_1_client_notification.md` (email to client)
- `touch_1_step_by_step.md` (guide for executing this touch)
- `prospects_researched.md` (all 10-15 prospects with details)

**ClickUp subtask:** "Touch 1 (Day 1): Free Intro Offer"
- Description contains: Step-by-step guide + links to all .md files

**NO PITCH** - Pure value delivery

---

### Touch 2 (Day 3): Loom Video - Show How You Create Intros

**Objective:** Creative credibility - show pain-signal tracking process

**What to create:**

1. Loom video script (3-5min)
   - Show how you found those prospects
   - Screen share: LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, or Apollo
   - Demo pain-signal filters
   - Explain 2-3 intro examples
   - End: "This is what you'd get on retainer - 15-25 intros/month"

2. Record video (manual step)

3. WhatsApp + Email messages
   - WhatsApp: "Hey {FirstName}! Recorded quick video showing how I found those {count} prospects..."
   - Email backup: Same message

**Deliverables (.md files):**
- `touch_2_video_script.md` (talking points for Loom)
- `touch_2_whatsapp_message.md` (WhatsApp copy)
- `touch_2_email_message.md` (Email backup copy)
- `touch_2_step_by_step.md` (guide: record video, send messages)

**ClickUp subtask:** "Touch 2 (Day 3): Loom Video - Intro Process"
- Description contains: Step-by-step guide + video script + message templates

**NO PITCH** - Educational transparency

---

### Touch 3 (Day 5-7): WhatsApp Intro Status Update

**Objective:** Check engagement, send more intros if client engaged

**What to do:**

1. Assess client engagement
   - Did they reply to Touch 1 or 2?
   - Did any prospects respond to intros?

2. **IF client engaged:**
   - Send 3-5 MORE intros (from remaining researched prospects)
   - WhatsApp: "Hey {FirstName} - checking in on those {count} intros. Have any responded? By the way, I have {count} more ready if you want them!"

3. **IF client didn't engage:**
   - One follow-up only
   - WhatsApp: "Hey {FirstName} - following up on the intros I sent. Did you get a chance to review? Any responses?"

**Deliverables (.md files):**
- `touch_3_additional_intros.md` (3-5 more intro emails, if engaged)
- `touch_3_whatsapp_engaged.md` (message if client engaged)
- `touch_3_whatsapp_no_engagement.md` (message if no response)
- `touch_3_step_by_step.md` (guide: assess engagement, send appropriate message)

**ClickUp subtask:** "Touch 3 (Day 5-7): WhatsApp Intro Status Update"
- Description contains: Engagement assessment criteria + message templates

**Decision point:** If NO engagement, consider stopping after Touch 5

---

### Touch 4 (Day 10): LinkedIn + Email Follow-Up

**Objective:** Multi-channel presence + case study social proof

**What to do:**

1. LinkedIn engagement
   - Like/comment on client's recent post
   - Wait 2-3 hours
   - LinkedIn DM: "Saw your post about {topic}! By the way, have those intros I sent turned into conversations?"

2. Email - Case study + infrastructure breakdown
   - Select case study matching client's industry
   - Explain Pain-Signal System infrastructure
   - Mention guarantee (8+ intros/month or work free)
   - Subject: "How I helped another {industry} company - {Case Study Name}"

**Deliverables (.md files):**
- `touch_4_linkedin_dm.md` (LinkedIn message)
- `touch_4_email_case_study.md` (Email with case study + system breakdown)
- `touch_4_step_by_step.md` (guide: LinkedIn engagement, send email)

**ClickUp subtask:** "Touch 4 (Day 10): LinkedIn + Email Follow-Up"
- Description contains: LinkedIn engagement steps + email template

**SOFT PITCH ALLOWED** - Introduce pricing, guarantee, system details

---

### Touch 5 (Day 12-14): Call Invitation

**Objective:** Final ask - invite to 15min discovery call

**What to send:**

1. WhatsApp message (primary)
   - Recap all value given (5-10 intros, video, follow-ups)
   - Explain Pain-Signal System benefits
   - Highlight guarantee
   - Ask for 15min call

2. Email (send 2 hours after WhatsApp if no response)
   - Same message as WhatsApp
   - More detailed breakdown
   - Include case study results

**Deliverables (.md files):**
- `touch_5_whatsapp_final_ask.md` (WhatsApp call invitation)
- `touch_5_email_final_ask.md` (Email call invitation)
- `touch_5_step_by_step.md` (guide: send messages, track response)

**ClickUp subtask:** "Touch 5 (Day 12-14): Call Invitation"
- Description contains: Final pitch templates + next steps

**Response tracking:**
- YES â†’ Schedule discovery call
- NO/IGNORE â†’ Mark "Cold - No Engagement", STOP nurturing

**FULL PITCH ALLOWED** - This is the final ask
