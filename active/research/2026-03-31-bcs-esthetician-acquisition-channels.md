# DECISION FACTORS — What Estheticians Evaluate Before Buying

When an esthetician is deciding whether to carry a new product line, they weigh these factors in order:

1. **Product efficacy + clinical backing** — Before/afters, clinical studies, ingredient data
2. **Profit margin + wholesale pricing** — Need 40–60%+ retail markup
3. **Training + education support** — In-person demos, protocol guides, ongoing support
4. **Minimum order requirements** — Low/no minimums win at acquisition stage
5. **Brand exclusivity** — They don't want products clients can buy on Amazon
6. **Packaging + shelf presence** — Must look premium enough to justify price
7. **Distributor relationship** — Responsiveness, shipping reliability, ongoing support

**BCS should lead messaging with:** "Health Canada compliant, Canadian shipping, training included, low minimums, professional-only distribution."
