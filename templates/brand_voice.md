# Beauty Connect Shop — Brand Voice Guide

## Tone
- **Professional** — We speak to aestheticians, clinic owners, and skincare professionals
- **Clean & Minimal** — Short sentences. No fluff. Every word earns its place.
- **Sophisticated** — Luxury positioning without being pretentious
- **Knowledgeable** — We know ingredients, protocols, and clinical results
- **Warm** — Professional but approachable. We're partners, not salespeople.

## Writing Rules
1. Lead with the benefit, not the feature
2. Use active voice: "Brightens skin in 4 weeks" not "Skin is brightened"
3. Keep headlines under 8 words
4. Keep body copy sentences under 20 words
5. Use "you/your" to address the reader directly
6. Avoid exclamation marks (max 1 per email)
7. No ALL CAPS except for promo codes

## Vocabulary
### Use These
- Professional-grade, clinical-grade, Korean dermaceutical
- Protocol, treatment, formulation
- Results-driven, evidence-based
- Skin barrier, microbiome, cellular renewal
- Curated, sourced directly from Korea

### Avoid These
- "Cheap", "budget", "affordable" (use "accessible" or "value")
- "Anti-aging" alone (use "slow aging" or "age management")
- "Miracle", "magic", "revolutionary"
- Therapeutic claims without qualification (Health Canada compliance)

## Health Canada Compliance
NEVER use these terms in email copy:
- "Heals", "repairs skin", "treats [condition]", "cures"
- "Anti-inflammatory" (unqualified), "anti-acne", "anti-fungal"
- "Stimulates [cellular/physiological process]"
- "Prevents disease", "medical-grade" (unless NPN registered)

SAFE alternatives:
- "Supports skin's natural renewal process"
- "Helps improve the appearance of..."
- "Formulated to help with..."
- "Designed for professional use"

## Email-Specific Guidelines
- Subject lines: 4-7 words, curiosity-driven or benefit-driven
- Preheader: Complement (don't repeat) the subject line
- Hero headline: Bold, benefit-focused, max 6 words
- CTA buttons: Action-oriented — "Shop Now", "Explore Collection", "Get Started"
- Sign-off: "The Beauty Connect Team" (not individual names)
